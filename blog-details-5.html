Literacy Ambassador for SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya

January 21, 2020
0 Comments

The Literacy Ambassador of SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya is something that never crossed my mind because I was appointed and trusted to be the representative of the literacy ambassador of SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya who will then compete with Literacy Ambassadors in All One High School in East Java province.

With a sense of pride and lack of confidence in the mandate appointed by the teacher at SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya, he followed several processes from beginning to end. But unfortunately, in the middle of the event, it turned out that the 3 Literacy Ambassadors from SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya were replaced with classmates because at that time I and my two friends who were entrusted to take part in a series of events turned out to coincide with school exams, practicum exams and try outs to face the national exam.

“With the life motto "fear kills more dreams than failure ever will" ” ― Nurul Aini

With the life motto that I have and I always hold on to to develop for the better in the future, of course this valuable experience can be used as a start for my journey to contribute many achievements wherever I am.
