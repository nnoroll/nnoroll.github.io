Champion Paskibraka SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya

January 21, 2020
0 Comments

Paskibraka SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya is one of the extracurricular activities at SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya. This extracurricular can be said to be one of the extracurriculars that SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya is proud of and relied on. Because this extracurricular got many achievements in competitions.

Of course, behind the achievements that have been made by the Paskibraka of SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya, which is the mainstay and pride in the school, there are students who spend quite a lot of time and energy to achieve an achievement in every competition.

“With the jargon that is owned "Who are we??? PASDASA..PASDASA..PASDASA.. Yeayy!"” ― Paskibraka SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya

With the jargon owned by the Paskibraka of SMA Negeri 10 Surabaya, he hopes that in the future he will continue to contribute achievements to his beloved school.
