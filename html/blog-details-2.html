Meet & Great First Time TSD'20

9 Sept 2020
0 Comments

Data Science for Your Bright Future: Data Science Technology, a new study program at UNAIR and the first in Indonesia that is trending internationally

The picture above is some of the faces of students of the first batch of data science technology study programs at Airlangga University. As it is known that this study program is a fresh program at Airlangga University. Then with the addition of a pandemic, the first batch of this new study program also could not meet in person, but for approximately 3 semesters which had been carried out following online learning. But that doesn't break the idea, of course, for this first batch to still meet face-to-face, even though it's not a full team or all of the data science technology students of the 2020 batch of 90, all in the context of friendship.

““Kuliah di TSD berbeda dengan kursus online, disini mahasiswa dapat praktek dengan bimbingan dosen. Selain itu, dengan menjadi mahasiswa maka dapat melatih jiwa disiplin dan tanggung jawab,” ” ― Pak Ruzza yang merupakan lulusan IOWA State University

As stated by Mr. Ruzza as a student lecturer of data science technology at Airlangga University, in fact online courses are not enough now, because later there will be different between lectures and only courses, both in terms of how we think, how to manage time, foster a leadership spirit and of course good relationships and experiences. from friends and experienced lecturers.
