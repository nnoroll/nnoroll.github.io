Event Project Team

6 Oct 2021
0 Comments

MAN Team is our name, consisting of Nurul Aini, Denise Arne, Mutiara Afifah who are students from data science technology at the Airlangga University 2020 who participated in the scientific writing competition of Airlangga Innovation Technology Competition 2021 on September 18, 2020

This is my first time with my friends to take part in a scientific writing competition. Even though I did not become a winner, for the first time participating in a scientific writing competition by qualifying as a grand finalist is something that I am proud of, although I am a little disappointed because I did not become a winner, but this makes me more enthusiastic and challenged to take part in other scientific writing competitions.
