UKM Kependudukan Universitas Airlangga

18 Oktober 2021
0 Comments

Universitas Airlangga Population UKM is a forum for Universitas Airlangga students who play a role in sustainable Population Insight Development in Indonesia.

Following the dynamics of population growth and empowerment efforts, Universitas Airlangga Population UKM held the 2019 Airlangga Population Festival. The event which was held in the Flamingo Restaurant Hall took the big theme of Millennial Generation Strategy in Overcoming Population Impacts to Realize SDG's 2030

“Indonesia this year's population growth rate is ranked 4th in the world. In a few years we hope to drop to rank 6.” ― Dr. Lutfi Agus Salim, SKM, M.Si selaku ahli demografi UNAIR

Therefore, as in the photo above, it is one of the work programs of the management of the Universitas Airlangga Population UKM for the 2020 - 2021 period, namely ADFEST (Airlangga Demography Festival) which is the largest annual event in this population SME with the aim of the millennial generation who continue and care about population development in Indonesia.
